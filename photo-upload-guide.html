📤 How to Upload Your Trip Photos

Teri, Lorene, Carla (and Joe) — takes about a minute a day. Photos you upload are sorted by day & place automatically each night and appear on the site's Photos tab, with geotagged shots pinned on the Map.

🎯 Where photos go

Everything goes into our one shared Google Drive folder:

Open the shared Photos folder
💡 One-time setup: in the Google Drive app, tap ⋮ next to the Photos folder and choose Add shortcut to Drive. Then it's always easy to find under My Drive.

Method A — Google Drive app easiest

Works the same on iPhone and Android.

  1. Open the Google Drive app and go to the Photos folder (My Drive → your shortcut).
  2. Tap the + New button → Upload → Photos and Videos.
  3. Select the day's photos and tap Upload. Done!

Method B — from the Google Photos app

  1. Long-press the first photo, then tap the rest to select the batch.
  2. Tap Share → Save to Drive (you may need to scroll the app row or tap More).
  3. Under Folder, tap and navigate to the shared Photos folder, then tap Save.

✅ Tips

  • Leave Location ON in your camera settings — that's what puts your photos on the trip map.
  • No renaming or sorting needed. The nightly automation figures out the day and place.
  • Upload over hotel Wi-Fi in the evening; new photos show up on the site by the next morning.
  • Big videos are fine to skip on slow Wi-Fi — photos first.
  • Uploaded the wrong shot? Just delete it from the Drive folder before 10 PM, or tell Joe.
